Our Mission

Hack3 is a global online hackathon that aims to enable high schools students to learn computer science. We believe that everyone can code and are always looking for ways to improve diversity within the computer technology space.

FAQ

What is Hack3?

Hack3 is a fun, 24 hour online hackathon made for high school students, both new and experienced. During this time, you will develop and present a project for cool prizes. Most importantly, you will learn new and useful skills that will help you on your computer science journey.

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where teams of people come together to design and build something involving a novel use of technology, usually centered around some theme.

What will happen during Hack3?

Expect team building activities, workshops led by industry professionals, and fun mini competitions!

Who should come to Hack3?

High school students with an interest in technology or programming.

How much experience do I need?

No experience is needed! Anyone with an interest in computer science can come and participate.

How many people can be in a team?

From 1 to 4.

Are there prizes?

Yes! There are prizes for first, second and third place for each of the three tracks, but you can only participate in one track.

How do I form a team?

We encourage you to form a team prior to Hack3, but we will host fun team formation activities too.

How is the winner determined?

A remote panel of experienced judges will thoroughly examine your project and grade it based on creativity, technical excellence, and theme relevance.
